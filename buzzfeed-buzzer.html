BuzzFeed: Buzzer

July 1st, 2020

BuzzFeed, Inc. is an American Internet media, news and entertainment company with a focus on digital media; it is based in New York City. BuzzFeed was founded in 2006 by Jonah Peretti and John S. Johnson III, to focus on tracking viral content.

Buzzer is a new feature that works similar to how users 'like' an article, but instead of likes icon, we use a buzzer icon to make it more fun.

Overview

  • Buzzer icon for readers to ‘like’ articles.
  • Project Duration: 3 months.
  • Budget: $200K (US).
  • Departments: Marketing, Design and Engineering.

KPIs

  • Additional 1 million users.
  • CAC: $1.03
  • NPS: 55+
  • 30%+ in user retention in 6 months after launch.
