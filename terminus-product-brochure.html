Terminus: Product Brochure

April 1st, 2016

Terminus Group was established with a team of ambitious technologists. Started out as a smart lock company, later it evolved its vision to build a platform that bridged the gap between AI and IoT, known as AIoT.

The new brochure designs as a children's story book, each product is a tri-fold page which opens up for more product detail. This design enables marketing team and sales team to make the product more vivid as they showcase to clients. The smart lock pictures in the tri-fold page are stickers, clients can peel it and attach to the lock outline on the next page, to have better visualization.

Overview

  • Story book style brochure.
  • Project Duration: 1 month.
  • Budget: $50,000 (US).
  • Departments: Marketing and Design.

Result

  • Clients requested over 2,000 copies.
  • JW Marriot hotels contacted us after receiving the brochure, and ordered 600 smart locks with sales team (partial credit).
