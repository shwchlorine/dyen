Jopwell: Job Assistant

May 22nd, 2020

Jopwell Launched in January 2015 by cofounders Porter Braswell and Ryan Williams. Companies using Jopwell have access to a digital pipeline of candidate profiles through which they can market job and internship opportunities and connect with users. The current user base includes Black, Latino/Hispanic, and Native American professionals and students. It aims to solve the "pipeline" problem wherein some companies' perceive that there aren't enough good candidates for diverse recruitment

Project Job Assistant is a Jopwell mobile application that allows users to track job listings, store personal information and resume, to make sure users can respond immediately when potential jobs are posted.

Overview

  • A mobile application as users' job search assistant.
  • Project Duration: 4 months.
  • Budget: $400,000 (US).
  • Departments: marketing, sales, support team, engineering.

KPIs

  • MRR: $200,000.
  • Additional 3 million signup users.
  • CAC: $1.43
  • NPS: 55+
  • 30%+ in user retention in 6 months after launch.
